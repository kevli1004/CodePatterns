Getting Started

Q: How do I start using CodePatterns?
A: Simply download the app from the App Store and open it. No account creation required! Your progress is automatically saved on your device.
Q: Is CodePatterns free?
A: CodePatterns offers both free and premium tiers. The free version includes core features like Battle Mode, Practice, Retry system, and Analytics. CodePatterns Premium is an auto-renewable monthly subscription ($4.99/month with 7-day free trial) that removes ads, provides unlimited explanations and battles, plus access to 200+ additional coding challenges.

CodePatterns Premium Subscription

CodePatterns Premium Details:
  • Price: $4.99 per month
  • Free Trial: 7 days free
  • Length: Auto-renewable monthly subscription (30 days)
  • Content: Ad-free experience, unlimited explanations, unlimited battles, advanced analytics, premium question bank
Q: How do I manage my CodePatterns Premium subscription?
To manage your subscription:
  1. Go to Settings on your iOS device
  2. Tap your name at the top
  3. Tap "Subscriptions"
  4. Select "CodePatterns Premium"
  5. You can cancel, modify, or view details here
You can also manage subscriptions directly through the App Store app.
Q: How do I cancel my subscription?
To cancel your CodePatterns Premium subscription:
  1. Open Settings on your iPhone or iPad
  2. Tap your name, then tap Subscriptions
  3. Tap CodePatterns Premium
  4. Tap "Cancel Subscription"
Your subscription will remain active until the end of the current billing period.
Q: I'm having trouble with my 7-day free trial. What should I do?
If you're experiencing issues with the free trial:
  1. Ensure you're signed into the same Apple ID that started the trial
  2. Check your internet connection during purchase
  3. Restart the app and try again
  4. Verify that the Paid Apps Agreement is accepted in your Apple ID account
  5. If the problem persists, contact support at codepatternsapp@gmail.com
Include your device model, iOS version, and a description of the issue.
