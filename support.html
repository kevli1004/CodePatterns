# CodePatterns - Support Documentation ## FAQ (Frequently Asked Questions) ### Getting Started **Q: How do I start using CodePatterns?** A: Simply download the app from the App Store and open it. No account creation required! Your progress is automatically saved on your device. **Q: Is CodePatterns free?** A: Yes! CodePatterns is completely free to download and use. All core features including Battle Mode, Practice, Retry system, and Analytics are available at no cost. **Q: Do I need an internet connection?** A: No! CodePatterns works completely offline. All questions, explanations, and features are available without an internet connection. ### Using the App **Q: What's the difference between Practice and Battle modes?** A: - **Practice Mode**: Learn at your own pace with detailed explanations after each question - **Battle Mode**: Real-time 30-second challenges against AI opponents for competitive practice **Q: How does the Retry system work?** A: The Retry system tracks questions you've answered incorrectly. Once you answer a question correctly (even in retry mode), it's marked as "mastered" and won't appear in your retry list again. **Q: What algorithm categories are covered?** A: CodePatterns covers 8+ essential categories: - Arrays & Hashing - Two Pointers - Sliding Window - Stacks & Queues - Binary Search - Linked Lists - Trees - Dynamic Programming - And more! **Q: How are the AI opponents different?** A: AI opponents have different skill levels: - **Beginner**: 65% accuracy, slower response times - **Intermediate**: 75% accuracy, moderate speed - **Advanced**: 85% accuracy, faster responses - **Expert**: 92% accuracy, very quick responses ### Progress and Achievements **Q: How is my progress tracked?** A: Your progress is tracked across multiple dimensions: - Questions answered correctly/incorrectly per category - Overall accuracy percentage - Time spent learning - Achievement badges earned - Battle wins and losses **Q: What achievements can I unlock?** A: There are multiple achievement categories: - **Learning**: "First Steps", "Warming Up", "On Fire" - **Mastery**: Category-specific expert badges - **Battle**: "First Victory", "Battle Veteran", "War Champion" - **Special**: "Perfect Run", "Point Master" **Q: Can I reset my progress?** A: Yes! Go to Profile → Settings → "Reset Progress" to start fresh. This action cannot be undone. ### Technical Issues **Q: The app is running slowly. What should I do?** A: Try these steps: 1. Close and reopen the app 2. Restart your device 3. Ensure you have the latest app version 4. Free up storage space on your device **Q: I lost my progress after updating. Can I get it back?** A: Progress is stored locally on your device. If you've lost progress after an update: 1. Check if you have an iCloud backup 2. Ensure you didn't accidentally reset progress in settings 3. Contact support if the issue persists **Q: The app crashes when I try to start a battle. Help!** A: This usually resolves with: 1. Force-close the app and reopen 2. Restart your device 3. Update to the latest version 4. If it continues, contact support with your device model and iOS version ### Account and Data **Q: Do I need to create an account?** A: No account creation is required. Your progress is automatically saved locally on your device. **Q: Can I sync progress across multiple devices?** A: Currently, progress is stored locally per device. Cloud sync may be added in future updates. **Q: Is my data safe and private?** A: Absolutely! Your data is stored locally on your device. We don't collect personal information or track your activity. See our Privacy Policy for full details. ### Contact and Support **Q: How do I report a bug or suggest a feature?** A: Contact us at support@codepatterns.app or use the feedback option in the app's Profile section. **Q: How often is the app updated?** A: We release updates regularly to fix bugs, improve performance, and add new features. Enable automatic updates in your App Store settings. --- ## Troubleshooting Guide ### Common Issues and Solutions #### App Won't Launch **Symptoms**: App crashes immediately upon opening **Solutions**: 1. Force-close the app: Double-tap home button (or swipe up), swipe up on CodePatterns 2. Restart your device completely 3. Update to latest iOS version 4. Reinstall the app (note: this will reset progress) #### Battle Mode Not Working **Symptoms**: Can't start battles, battles freeze, timer doesn't work **Solutions**: 1. Close and reopen the app 2. Check available storage space (need at least 100MB free) 3. Update the app to latest version 4. Reset battle mode: Profile → Settings → "Reset Launch Screen" #### Progress Not Saving **Symptoms**: Achievements reset, scores disappear **Solutions**: 1. Ensure app has storage permissions 2. Don't force-close app during quiz sessions 3. Allow app to fully close before switching apps 4. Check device storage space #### Performance Issues **Symptoms**: Slow animations, laggy interface, delayed responses **Solutions**: 1. Close other running apps 2. Restart device 3. Free up storage space 4. Update iOS to latest version 5. Check device compatibility (iOS 14.0+ required) --- ## System Requirements ### Minimum Requirements - **Device**: iPhone 6s or newer, iPad (6th generation) or newer - **iOS**: iOS 14.0 or later - **Storage**: 50MB free space - **RAM**: 2GB (recommended 3GB+) ### Recommended Requirements - **Device**: iPhone 8 or newer, iPad Pro or iPad Air - **iOS**: iOS 15.0 or later - **Storage**: 100MB free space - **RAM**: 3GB or more ### Compatibility Notes - Optimized for all iPhone and iPad screen sizes - Supports Dark Mode (follows system setting) - VoiceOver accessibility support - Landscape and portrait orientations --- ## Version History and Known Issues ### Current Version: 1.0.0 **Features**: - Complete algorithm learning system - Real-time Battle Mode - Smart Retry system - Comprehensive analytics - Achievement system **Known Issues**: - None currently reported ### Reporting New Issues If you discover a bug not listed here, please report it to support@codepatterns.app with: - Steps to reproduce - Expected vs. actual behavior - Device and app version information - Screenshots if applicable --- **We're committed to providing excellent support and continuously improving CodePatterns based on your feedback!**
