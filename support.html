Getting Started

Q: How do I start using CodePatterns?
A: Simply download the app from the App Store and open it. No account creation required! Your progress is automatically saved on your device.
Q: Is CodePatterns free?
A: Yes! CodePatterns is completely free to download and use. All core features including Battle Mode, Practice, Retry system, and Analytics are available at no cost.
Q: Do I need an internet connection?
A: No! CodePatterns works completely offline. All questions, explanations, and features are available without an internet connection.

Using the App

Q: What's the difference between Practice and Battle modes?
Practice Mode: Learn at your own pace with detailed explanations after each question
Battle Mode: Real-time 30-second challenges against AI opponents for competitive practice
Q: How does the Retry system work?
The Retry system tracks questions you've answered incorrectly. Once you answer a question correctly (even in retry mode), it's marked as "mastered" and won't appear in your retry list again.
Q: What algorithm categories are covered?
CodePatterns covers 8+ essential categories:
  • Arrays & Hashing
  • Two Pointers
  • Sliding Window
  • Stacks & Queues
  • Binary Search
  • Linked Lists
  • Trees
  • Dynamic Programming
  • And more!
Q: How are the AI opponents different?
AI opponents have different skill levels:
  • Beginner: 65% accuracy, slower response times
  • Intermediate: 75% accuracy, moderate speed
  • Advanced: 85% accuracy, faster responses
  • Expert: 92% accuracy, very quick responses

Progress and Achievements

Q: How is my progress tracked?
Your progress is tracked across multiple dimensions:
  • Questions answered correctly/incorrectly per category
  • Overall accuracy percentage
  • Time spent learning
  • Achievement badges earned
  • Battle wins and losses
Q: What achievements can I unlock?
There are multiple achievement categories:
  • Learning: "First Steps", "Warming Up", "On Fire"
  • Mastery: Category-specific expert badges
  • Battle: "First Victory", "Battle Veteran", "War Champion"
  • Special: "Perfect Run", "Point Master"

Common Issues and Solutions

App Won't Launch

Symptoms: App crashes immediately upon opening
Solutions:
  1. Force-close the app: Double-tap home button (or swipe up), swipe up on CodePatterns
  2. Restart your device completely
  3. Update to latest iOS version
  4. Reinstall the app (note: this will reset progress)

Battle Mode Not Working

Symptoms: Can't start battles, battles freeze, timer doesn't work
Solutions:
  1. Close and reopen the app
  2. Check available storage space (need at least 100MB free)
  3. Update the app to latest version
  4. Reset battle mode: Profile â†’ Settings â†’ "Reset Launch Screen"

Progress Not Saving

Symptoms: Achievements reset, scores disappear
Solutions:
  1. Ensure app has storage permissions
  2. Don't force-close app during quiz sessions
  3. Allow app to fully close before switching apps
  4. Check device storage space

Performance Issues

Symptoms: Slow animations, laggy interface, delayed responses
Solutions:
  1. Close other running apps
  2. Restart device
  3. Free up storage space
  4. Update iOS to latest version
  5. Check device compatibility (iOS 14.0+ required)
